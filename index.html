Integrations

Complement your web.

Our tools provide you excellent facilities:

  • Bind JavaScript functions to keys; create keybinds!
  • Access our pre-made flexible JavaScript functions!
  • Construct your website in minutes!
  • We offer minified files too, save storage!
  • Beautiful and meticulous errors messages in console for some of our integrations!
  • Improve vocabulary on your website!
  • Apply smoother effects!
  • Access our free icon base, the icons are stored in our file system, save your storage!

Which ones should I pick?

ConstructJS    Create your website faster with minimized code.
Bind    Bind keys on the keyboard to execute JavaScript code, flexible.
Swiftstyle    Style your website faster and smoother.
Datascope    Access XML/JSON via XHRs (XMLHttpRequests), or our pre-set database.
Vocabulary    Improve the vocabulary displayed on the page.
Iconic    Add iconic icons to your site, preferably for UI. Adds ability to add images inside text, inline; good for custom emojis.
